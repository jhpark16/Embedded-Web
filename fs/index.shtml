LED Control Battery Voltage

This page allows you to control the four LEDs: LED1, LED2, LED3 and LED4 located in the black STM32F407VET board. To put on/off a LED you have to check/uncheck its corresponding checkbox. Then you have to click on "Send" button to submit the new LEDs configuration.
